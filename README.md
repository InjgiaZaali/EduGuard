# EduGuardian — AI Study & Exam Helper

EduGuardian is a student-focused technology project that solves a real-world problem: students often have too many notes, PDFs, and lecture materials, but they do not know what is important or what to revise first.

This prototype helps students by turning study material into:

- simple summaries
- practice quiz questions
- flashcards
- weak-topic tracking
- a revision plan before the exam

## Problem

Many students feel stressed before exams because their study materials are large and unorganized. They may read everything again and again, but still not know which topics they understand and which topics are weak.

## Solution

EduGuardian works like a personal AI study coach. A student pastes lecture notes or uploads a text file. The app analyzes the text and generates learning tools. After the quiz, it tracks wrong answers and recommends what to revise first.

## Main Features

1. **Study Material Input**
   - Paste notes directly into the app.
   - Upload a `.txt` file.

2. **Simple Summary**
   - Extracts important sentences.
   - Shows main topics and keywords.

3. **Quiz Generator**
   - Creates multiple-choice questions based on detected topics.
   - Checks answers instantly.

4. **Weak Area Detection**
   - Wrong answers are saved as weak topics.
   - Students can revise weak topics first.

5. **Flashcards**
   - Creates clickable flashcards from important keywords.
   - Helps with active recall.

6. **Study Plan**
   - Creates a day-by-day revision plan based on exam date and daily study time.

## Technology Used

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript
- Browser LocalStorage

This is a frontend prototype, so it works without installation and without a backend server.

## How to Run

1. Download or open the project folder.
2. Open `index.html` in a web browser.
3. Click **Load demo notes** or paste your own notes.
4. Try summary, quiz, flashcards, and study plan.

## Demo Script

Use this during presentation:

1. Open the EduGuardian website.
2. Click **Load demo notes**.
3. Click **Create summary** and show the main ideas.
4. Click **Generate quiz** and answer some questions incorrectly.
5. Click **Check answers** and show that weak topics are detected.

7. Click **Build study plan** and show the revision plan.

## Innovation

The project is innovative because it does not only summarize notes. It also tests the student, remembers weak areas, and creates a personalized plan. This makes studying more active and less stressful.
